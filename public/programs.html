Partner Ecosystem

We work alongside other incredible organizations in Sri Lanka to ensure youth have multiple pathways to success.

Organization & Program Core Focus Best Suited For
SLASSCOM Future Careers BRIDGE Free virtual bootcamps focused on soft skills, project management, and internships in tech & BPM. Students aiming for IT, tech, and BPM careers.
ICTA Lighting Digital Youth Practical digital skills training including web design, content creation, and freelancing. Regional school leavers exploring digital entrepreneurship.
CIPM Sri Lanka Employability Readiness 10-week professional certification covering ethics, business structures, and job hunting. Students seeking HR and business certifications.
CareerMe School Portals & Workshops Career assessments, degree selectors, and multilingual counseling programs. Students unsure about their career direction.

⚡ The Reality Check

In today's job market, strong A/L results alone are not enough. Employers prioritize communication, adaptability, collaboration, and digital fluency. A student with professional agility and practical workplace skills will consistently outperform someone with perfect grades but zero real-world readiness.
