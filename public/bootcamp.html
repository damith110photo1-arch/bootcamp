📅 Program Structure

4-Week Roadmap

Week 1

Foundation & Identity

We start by building your professional identity. Create a standout resume, optimize your LinkedIn profile, and learn the unwritten rules of corporate communication.

Week 2

Digital Fluency & Tools

Master the tools used by modern IT teams. Get hands-on experience with Slack, Google Workspace, GitHub basics, and using AI efficiently to accelerate your work.

Week 3

Industry Deep-Dive

Understand the Sri Lankan tech landscape. Learn about different roles, career pathways, salary expectations, and what employers are actually looking for.

Week 4

Simulation & Placement

The final week is all about practice. Engage in mock interviews, group assessments, and technical challenges with real industry professionals giving live feedback.
